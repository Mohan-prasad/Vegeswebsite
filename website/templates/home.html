About Us

We are local farmers cultivating our vegetables using time-honoured, sustainable practices using Jeevamrutam, Ganajeevamrutam, and Panchagavya — potent natural formulations that enrich the soil, enhance plant vitality, and naturally deter pests, all without the use of chemicals. These traditional methods yield vegetables that are nutrient-dense, flavourful, and entirely safe for your family. We harvest to order and deliver directly from our farm to your doorstep — eliminating middlemen and extended storage. This ensures you receive vegetables that are consistently fresh, crisp, and full of flavour. Our farm-to-home delivery service guarantees seasonal produce at its peak quality, straight from our fields to your kitchen.
Choosing chemical-free vegetables is not merely an option — it is essential.
